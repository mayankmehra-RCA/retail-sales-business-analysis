# Business Insights – Retail Sales Analysis

## Project Objective
The objective of this project was to analyze retail sales data and identify patterns in revenue, profitability, product performance, and regional sales distribution.

The analysis was performed using SQL and Excel to simulate real-world business analyst workflows.

---

# Dataset Overview
The dataset contains 9995 sales transactions with the following attributes:

- Ship Mode
- Segment
- Country
- City
- State
- Region
- Category
- Sub-Category
- Sales
- Quantity
- Discount
- Profit

---

# Key Insights

## 1. Regional Performance
The West region generates the highest sales and profit for the business.

This indicates strong customer demand and successful product distribution in this region.

## 2. Category Performance
The Technology category generates the highest revenue among all product categories.

This suggests technology products are the primary revenue drivers for the business.

## 3. Product Profitability
The most profitable sub-categories include:

- Phones
- Chairs
- Storage

These products contribute significantly to overall business profitability.

## 4. Low Performing Products
Some sub-categories generate very low sales and profit.

For example:
- Fasteners show extremely low revenue contribution.

This suggests low demand or poor pricing strategy.

## 5. Discount Impact
Higher discounts were observed to reduce profitability.

This indicates that aggressive discounting strategies negatively affect profit margins.

---

# Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations can improve business performance:

1. Focus marketing efforts on high performing regions such as West.

2. Increase inventory and promotion for high profit sub-categories like Phones and Chairs.

3. Review discount strategies to avoid unnecessary profit loss.

4. Re-evaluate low performing products to determine whether they should be improved or removed from the catalog.
